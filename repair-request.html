Gas smell: open doors and windows, avoid flames and electrical switches, then call the Cadent gas emergency line 0800 111 999. You can message us on 07312 126456 afterwards for local follow-up once you are safe.

Urgent water escape: call or text 07312 126456 or 01524 409398.
