Breakdowns

Repair call out

If your boiler, fire or heating system is faulty or showing an error, use our repair request form so we can assess the job and respond appropriately. Routine annual servicing is usually arranged via our servicing page.

Gas smell or major leak

If you smell gas, open doors and windows, avoid naked flames and switches, and call the Cadent gas emergency line on 0800 111 999. Message us on 07312 126456 for local follow-up once you are safe.

Water leaks

For urgent water leaks, call or text 07312 126456 or 01524 409398 so we can advise or attend as agreed.

Standard repair request

For faults, non-emergency leaks, loss of heating or unusual appliance behaviour — submit a call out request.
